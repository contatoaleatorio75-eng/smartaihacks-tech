### The Hybrid Strategy

It's not about not using AI, but about using it the right way.

1.  **AI for Structure, Human for Soul:** Let the AI build the skeleton of your article or video. Let it do the raw research. But **you** must write the introduction, the personal anecdotes, and the conclusion.
2.  **Opinion is King:** AI is neutral by nature. Humans have opinions. Be provocative. Pick a side. Share a failure. This creates a real connection that an algorithm can never replicate.
3.  **The "Rough Edges":** Don't try to polish everything to absolute perfection. A slightly shaky camera or a more casual tone of voice prove that you are real.

Use AI to be efficient, not to be lazy. The winners of 2026 are those who use high-tech tools to deliver high-quality humanity. At the end of the day, people buy from people, not from bots.
